Why Jump with us?

We're situated in the heart of the Cape Winelands, nestled in the scenic farmlands of Wellington. Your tandem skydive should be a fun-filled, unforgettable experience and the best introduction to skydiving available. We pride ourselves on making sure that everything from booking until the moment your feet touch the earth again is a first-class experience.


With over 45 years combined experience and with an impressive total of over 43 000 skydives between us, we invite you to step out of your comfort zone, and into our world of skydiving.
